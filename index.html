Simplifying Tipout

Our goal is to streamline tip distribution by providing servers and bartenders with a user-friendly platform that simplifies the process of tipping out restaurant staff.

Why Use TipAssist?

Speed

TipAssist enables servers and bartenders to effortlessly calculate tipout to restaurant staff, accelerating the tip-out process with just a few clicks.

Customizable

Allows for easy and flexible adjustment of varying tipout percentages to meet the specific needs of your restaurant tipout process.

Consistent

Ensures accurate and consistent tipout calculations by providing a reliable platform that eliminates errors and ensures precise distribution of tips
